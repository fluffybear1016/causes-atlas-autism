# COM-0001 Leucovorin + Methyl-B12 + Sulforaphane


No published RCT of the triple. Each component has positive RCT individually. Anecdotal reports (ANE-0010) suggest synergistic effect. High priority for first-class combo investigation.


## Rationale

Targets cerebral folate deficiency (leucovorin), methylation cycle (B12), and oxidative stress / NRF2 (sulforaphane) simultaneously — three independent mechanisms commonly co-occurring in autism phenotypes.


## Interaction warnings

Generally safe; monitor for excess methylation symptoms in COMT++ children.
