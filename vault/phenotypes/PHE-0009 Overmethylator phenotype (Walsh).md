# PHE-0009 Overmethylator phenotype (Walsh)


High whole-blood histamine, high SAM/SAH ratio. Personality cluster: anxious, low motivation, paradoxical histamine elevation. ~5-10% of autism per Walsh. Treatment: folate-cautious; niacinamide, B6, sometimes folate-blocking strategies.


**Diagnostic markers:** High whole-blood histamine; high SAM/SAH ratio


**Notes:** Per Walsh framework. Methyl donors can paradoxically worsen.
