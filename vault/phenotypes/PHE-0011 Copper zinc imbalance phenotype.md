# PHE-0011 Copper zinc imbalance phenotype


Elevated free copper + low zinc (Cu:Zn >1.2 per Walsh framework). Hyperactivity, sensory issues, behavioral dysregulation. Postpartum depression risk in mothers. Treatment: zinc supplementation, molybdenum, metallothionein-promoting protocols.


**Diagnostic markers:** Elevated plasma copper; low plasma zinc; Cu:Zn ratio >1.2
