# BIO-0020 — Lactate-to-pyruvate ratio (L:P)

**Category:** mitochondrial / oxphos_indicator

**Sample type:** plasma  
**Units:** ratio  
**Reference range:** 10–25  

**What it measures:** Most sensitive biochemical screen for respiratory-chain deficiency

**Elevated means:** L:P > 25 suggests OXPHOS / respiratory chain dysfunction

**Interpretation:** More informative than lactate alone; primary screen for mito disease
