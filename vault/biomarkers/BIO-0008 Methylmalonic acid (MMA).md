# BIO-0008 — Methylmalonic acid (MMA)

**Category:** methylation_cycle / b12_functional

**Sample type:** serum_or_urine  
**Units:** nmol/L  
**Reference range:** 0–270  

**What it measures:** Functional B12 status — elevated when B12 cofactor activity is insufficient regardless of plasma B12 level

**Elevated means:** Functional B12 deficiency even if plasma B12 normal

**Interpretation:** More sensitive than plasma B12 alone for cellular B12 status
