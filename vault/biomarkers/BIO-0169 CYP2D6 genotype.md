# BIO-0169 — CYP2D6 genotype

**Category:** genetic_snp / pharmacogenomic

**Sample type:** buccal_or_blood  
**Units:** phenotype (poor/intermediate/normal/rapid/ultra-rapid metabolizer)  

**What it measures:** CYP2D6 drug-metabolism variant — affects SSRI, antipsychotic, atomoxetine, codeine response

**Interpretation:** Critical pre-prescription test for autistic kids being prescribed psychotropics. Ultra-rapid metabolizers can have toxic effects at standard doses; poor metabolizers can have no effect.

**SNP dependence:** self

- Availability: standard_clinical
- CLIA: CLIA
- Labs: GeneSight, Genomind, Tempus PGx, standard clinical labs
