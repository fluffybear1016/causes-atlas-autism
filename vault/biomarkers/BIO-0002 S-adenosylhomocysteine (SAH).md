# BIO-0002 — S-adenosylhomocysteine (SAH)

**Category:** methylation_cycle

**Sample type:** plasma  
**Units:** nmol/L  
**Reference range:** 10–30  

**What it measures:** Methyltransferase product; rises when methylation outflow blocked

**Elevated means:** Methylation-cycle inhibition; usually paired with low SAM/SAH ratio

**Low means:** Generally non-pathological


## Test logistics
- Availability: commercial
- Labs: Doctor's Data, Genova
- Cost: $80–$180 USD
