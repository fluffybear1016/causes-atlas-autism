# BIO-0003 — SAM/SAH ratio

**Category:** methylation_cycle / methylation_capacity

**Sample type:** plasma  
**Units:** ratio  
**Reference range:** 3.0–6.0  
**Optimal range:** 4.0–6.0  

**What it measures:** Net methylation capacity — the single most informative methylation biomarker

**Elevated means:** Overmethylator state; folate caution

**Low means:** Impaired methylation; undermethylator state; leucovorin/methyl-B12 candidate

**SNP dependence:** MTHFR C677T, MTRR, MTR, BHMT all affect interpretation


## Test logistics
- Availability: commercial
- Labs: Doctor's Data DDI methylation panel
