# BIO-0017 — CSF 5-MTHF

**Category:** folate_receptor / cerebral_folate_direct

**Sample type:** cerebrospinal_fluid  
**Units:** nmol/L  
**Reference range:** 40–180  

**What it measures:** Direct measurement of cerebral folate; gold-standard for cerebral folate deficiency diagnosis

**Low means:** Cerebral folate deficiency confirmed; high-dose leucovorin indicated

**Interpretation:** Invasive (lumbar puncture); reserved for confirmed clinical suspicion. CSF/serum ratio <1 is diagnostic for transport defect.
