# BIO-0015 — FRAA (folate receptor alpha autoantibody) — blocking

**Category:** folate_receptor / autoantibody

**Sample type:** serum  
**Units:** pmol/mL FBA  
**Reference range:** 0–0.5  

**What it measures:** Autoantibody blocking folate transport across blood-brain barrier

**Elevated means:** Cerebral folate deficiency likely; leucovorin candidate

**Interpretation:** The single most important biomarker for predicting leucovorin response in autism


## Test logistics
- Availability: specialty_research_lab
- Labs: Religious Sisters of Mercy / Ramaekers lab; some commercial
- Cost: $200–$400 USD
