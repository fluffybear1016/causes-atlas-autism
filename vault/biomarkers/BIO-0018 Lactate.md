# BIO-0018 — Lactate

**Category:** mitochondrial / anaerobic_shift

**Sample type:** plasma  
**Units:** mmol/L  
**Reference range:** 0.5–2.2  

**What it measures:** Anaerobic metabolism marker; rises when oxidative phosphorylation can't meet ATP demand

**Elevated means:** Mitochondrial dysfunction; pyruvate dehydrogenase deficiency; respiratory chain defect

**Interpretation:** Compare resting vs post-exertion; resting elevation more concerning
