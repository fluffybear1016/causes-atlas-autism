# BIO-0004 — Plasma methionine

**Category:** methylation_cycle

**Sample type:** plasma  
**Units:** μmol/L  
**Reference range:** 15–40  

**What it measures:** Methionine cycle substrate; derived from dietary intake + remethylation

**Elevated means:** Possible MAT defect (rare) or supplementation

**Low means:** Methionine cycle compromise; consider methyl-B12 + betaine
