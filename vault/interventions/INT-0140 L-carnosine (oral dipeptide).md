# INT-0140 L-carnosine (oral dipeptide)


Beta-alanyl-L-histidine dipeptide. GABA modulation via homocarnosine pathway; possible anticonvulsant + frontal/temporal cortex protective effects. Used by Chez 2002 J Child Neurol RCT (PMID 12585724) at 800 mg/day in autism.


**Dose range:** 800 mg/day (Chez 2002 dose)


**Cost per month (USD):** 20


**Notes / warnings:** Added 2026-05-09 to fix rrc_005 cohort entry. Distinct molecule from L-carnitine (INT-0011). FRM-0047 reparented to this entry.


## Evidence balance


### Mainstream consensus position


> Small pediatric RCT (Chez 2002, n=31) showed significant improvement on Gilliam ARS + Receptive Vocabulary; not replicated at scale.
