# INT-0143 IVIG (intravenous immunoglobulin)


Pooled-donor immunoglobulin G concentrate. Anti-inflammatory + immunomodulatory in autoimmune neuropsychiatric conditions. Used in PANS/PANDAS subset of autism per Frankovich 2017 PANS Research Consortium guidelines.


**Dose range:** 1.0-2.0 g/kg per infusion course; cycle schedule varies


**Cost per month (USD):** 5000


**Notes / warnings:** Added 2026-05-09 to fix rrc_013 cohort entry. Specialist intervention; thrombotic + aseptic-meningitis risk. PANS-overlap subset of autism.


## Evidence balance


### Mainstream consensus position


> Perlmutter 1999 PANDAS RCT showed OC symptom reduction. Williams 2016 PANDAS RCT did not reach binary statistical significance; open-label IVIG ~50% OCD severity reduction. Frankovich 2017 guidelines list IVIG as preferred treatment for moderate-to-severe PANS.
