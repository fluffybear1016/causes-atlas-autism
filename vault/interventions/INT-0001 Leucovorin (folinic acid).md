# INT-0001 Leucovorin (folinic acid)


Reduced folate that bypasses folate-receptor autoantibody blockade; restores cerebral folate transport. Crosses BBB.


**Dose range:** 0.5–2 mg/kg/day (max 50 mg/day)


**Cost per month (USD):** 60


**Notes / warnings:** Calibration target: CSRS > 80


## Hypotheses

- [[HYP-0001 FOLR1 autoantibodies cerebral folate deficiency]]

## Mechanisms

- [[MEC-0003 Impaired methylation]]
- [[MEC-0004 BBB dysfunction]]

## Genes

- `GEN-0001 FOLR1` (see [[INDEX|genes/INDEX]])

## Phenotypes

- [[PHE-0001 Cerebral folate deficiency phenotype]]

## Combinations

- [[COM-0001 Leucovorin + Methyl-B12 + Sulforaphane]]
- [[COM-0006 Leucovorin + Methyl-B12 + Vitamin D]]
