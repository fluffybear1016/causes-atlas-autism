# INT-0092 Hyperbaric oxygen therapy (HBOT)


Pressurized oxygen (1.3-1.5 ATA) delivered via chamber. Hypothesized to address tissue hypoxia, reduce neuroinflammation, and modulate cerebral blood flow. Evidence mixed — Rossignol 2009 multicenter RCT (n=62) reported modest improvements, Granpeesheh 2010 trial null, meta-analyses suggest possible subset response.


**Dose range:** 40 sessions of 45-60 min @ 1.3 ATA


**Cost per month (USD):** 5000


**Notes / warnings:** Genuinely contested; positive in subset, negative overall in some RCTs. Per spec §1.1 included with honest provenance.
