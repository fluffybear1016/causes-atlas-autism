# INT-0119 PQQ (pyrroloquinoline quinone)


Mitochondrial biogenesis promoter


**Dose range:** 10-20 mg/day


**Notes / warnings:** Pyrroloquinoline quinone — promotes mitochondrial biogenesis (creates new mitochondria, not just supports existing function). Generally well-tolerated in pediatric supplementation; minimal published autism-specific clinical trials. Mechanism via PGC-1α activation.
