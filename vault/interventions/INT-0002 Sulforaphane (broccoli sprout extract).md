# INT-0002 Sulforaphane (broccoli sprout extract)


Activates NRF2 antioxidant response; upregulates phase II detox enzymes; reduces oxidative stress associated with autism phenotypes.


**Dose range:** 50–200 µmol/day


**Cost per month (USD):** 40


**Notes / warnings:** Singh 2014 RCT in adolescent males
