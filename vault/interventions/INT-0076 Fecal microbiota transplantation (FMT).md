# INT-0076 Fecal microbiota transplantation (FMT)


Whole microbiota transfer from healthy donor (often familial). Kang 2017 open-label MTT showed sustained 45% reduction in ASD symptoms 2 years post; Hazan 2024 familial sibling FMT case report showed nonverbal → verbal emergence with Bifidobacterium restoration.


**Dose range:** Single 300 mL infusion or multi-dose over 8 weeks


**Cost per month (USD):** 5000


**Notes / warnings:** Kang 2017+2019 sustained outcomes; Hazan 2024.
