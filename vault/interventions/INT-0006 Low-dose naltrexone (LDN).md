# INT-0006 Low-dose naltrexone (LDN)


Sub-clinical doses produce paradoxical immunomodulation; reduces neuroinflammation; case reports in ASD.


**Dose range:** 0.5–4.5 mg at bedtime


**Cost per month (USD):** 35


**Notes / warnings:** Limited RCT data; broader off-label use
