# INT-0128 Trehalose (autophagy promoter)


Disaccharide; promotes autophagy and mitophagy


**Dose range:** 5-15 g/day


**Notes / warnings:** Disaccharide that promotes autophagy / mitophagy via mTOR-independent pathway. Clearance of damaged mitochondria + protein aggregates. Emerging research in neurodegenerative + neurodevelopmental contexts. Generally well-tolerated; some GI tolerance issues at higher doses.
