# INT-0008 Methylated folate (5-MTHF) — preconception prenatal


Bioavailable form of folate that bypasses MTHFR variants; preconception/prenatal supplementation associated with reduced autism risk in some cohorts.


**Dose range:** 400–800 µg/day preconception + pregnancy


**Cost per month (USD):** 15


**Notes / warnings:** Surén 2013 MoBa cohort
