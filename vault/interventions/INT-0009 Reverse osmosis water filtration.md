# INT-0009 Reverse osmosis water filtration


Removes >99% of PFAS/glyphosate/heavy metals from drinking water; reduces prenatal and childhood exposure to known endocrine disruptors and neurotoxicants.


**Dose range:** One-time install $200-500


**Cost per month (USD):** 20


**Notes / warnings:** See Miami Beach PFAS memo for documented exposure pathway
