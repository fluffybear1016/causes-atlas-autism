# INT-0086 Phospholipid DHA (krill oil salmon roe)


Phospholipid form of DHA; ~10x preferential brain accumulation vs triglyceride DHA. Patrick emphasizes for pregnancy/lactation and developmental windows. Werner 2011 demonstrated fold-higher brain DHA from phospholipid form.


**Dose range:** 500-2000 mg combined EPA+DHA in PL form/day


**Cost per month (USD):** 50


**Notes / warnings:** Distinct from INT-0014 standard fish oil.
