# INT-0097 Red light photobiomodulation therapy (635 nm)


Transcranial red laser (635 nm, 5 min, 2x/week, 4 weeks) applied at skull base/temples. Leisman 2018 double-blind RCT (n=21-22) showed ~50% reductions in irritability, lethargy/social withdrawal, stereotypic behavior, hyperactivity, and total ABC-C score vs. placebo LED, with gains persisting. Mechanism: photobiomodulation activates cytochrome c oxidase, boosts ATP, reduces neuroinflammation.


**Dose range:** 635 nm laser, 5 min @ skull base + temples, 2x/week, 4 weeks


**Cost per month (USD):** 200


**Notes / warnings:** Small but rigorous RCT; replication needed. Outdoctrination X clip referenced this study.
