# INT-0066 Iodine (potassium iodide kelp)


Iodine repletion to support thyroid hormone synthesis during pregnancy and lactation; preventable cause of neurodevelopmental impairment.


**Dose range:** 150-220 mcg/day prenatal; 90 mcg child


**Cost per month (USD):** 8


**Notes / warnings:** WHO recommends universal salt iodization.
