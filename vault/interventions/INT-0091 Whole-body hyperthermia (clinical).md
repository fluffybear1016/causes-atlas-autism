# INT-0091 Whole-body hyperthermia (clinical)


Medically supervised raising of core body temperature to ~38.5°C. Activates HSF1/HSP70, modulates immune response. Investigational for autism per fever-effect rationale.


**Dose range:** Single 90-min session, core temp 38.5°C


**Cost per month (USD):** 500


**Notes / warnings:** Distinct from home sauna (INT-0048); investigational.
