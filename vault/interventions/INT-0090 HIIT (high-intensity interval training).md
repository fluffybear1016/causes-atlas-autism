# INT-0090 HIIT (high-intensity interval training)


High-intensity intervals produce more lactate (cf. Brooks lactate shuttle), driving more BDNF crossing BBB. Patrick + Gibala emphasize for cognitive/neuroplasticity benefit. Distinct from steady-state aerobic (INT-0047).


**Dose range:** Tabata 4 min, or 4x4 min @ 90% HR_max, 2-3x/week


**Cost per month (USD):** 0


**Notes / warnings:** Pediatric adaptation needed; very modifiable.
