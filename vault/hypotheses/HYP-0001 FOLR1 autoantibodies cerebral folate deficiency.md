# HYP-0001 FOLR1 autoantibodies cerebral folate deficiency


Maternal or postnatal autoantibodies to FOLR1 block folate transport across blood-brain barrier; causes cerebral folate deficiency phenotype responsive to high-dose folinic acid.


**Affected population:** ~10-25% of cases (subset)


**Notes:** Frye et al. multiple replications


## Mechanisms

- [[MEC-0003 Impaired methylation]]
- [[MEC-0004 BBB dysfunction]]

## Interventions

- [[INT-0001 Leucovorin (folinic acid)]]

## Genes

- `GEN-0001 FOLR1` (see [[INDEX|genes/INDEX]])
