# HYP-0056 Bifidobacterium depletion (autism-specific)


Multi-meta-analyses show consistent reduction in Bifidobacterium abundance (esp. B. longum, B. breve, B. infantis) in autism vs. neurotypical children. Bifidobacteria produce GABA, modulate immune tone, protect epithelial integrity. Hazan 2024 case documents Bifidobacterium 0% → 1.56% post-FMT with verbal emergence.


**Affected population:** Population-wide signal in autism cohorts


**Notes:** Iglesias-Vázquez 2020 meta-analysis n=1972.
