# HYP-0010 Prenatal valproate (sodium valproate) exposure


Valproate during pregnancy causes ~10x increased autism risk; HDAC inhibitor mechanism; FDA warning; widely used as ASD model in animal research.


**Affected population:** Children of women on valproate during pregnancy


**Notes:** Christensen 2013 cohort
