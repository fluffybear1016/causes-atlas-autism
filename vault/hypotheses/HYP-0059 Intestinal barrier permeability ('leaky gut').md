# HYP-0059 Intestinal barrier permeability ('leaky gut')


Elevated zonulin, lactulose-mannitol ratio, and serum LPS reported in autism. Permits microbial metabolite translocation and chronic immune activation. Distinct from microbiome composition itself.


**Affected population:** ~25-50% of autism cases


**Notes:** de Magistris 2010.
