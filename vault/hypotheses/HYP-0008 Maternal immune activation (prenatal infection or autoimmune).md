# HYP-0008 Maternal immune activation (prenatal infection or autoimmune)


Maternal infection or autoimmune activation during pregnancy releases cytokines that cross placenta and disrupt fetal neurodevelopment; replicated in animal models and epidemiological studies.


**Affected population:** Variable by exposure


**Notes:** Estes & McAllister 2016 review
