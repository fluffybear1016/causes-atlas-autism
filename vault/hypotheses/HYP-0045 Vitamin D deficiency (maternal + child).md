# HYP-0045 Vitamin D deficiency (maternal + child)


Low maternal 25-OH-D associated with autism risk; supplementation in deficient children improves behavior. Modifies immune and calcium signaling.


**Affected population:** Latitude-dependent; ~30-50% deficient


**Notes:** Saad 2018 RCT positive.
