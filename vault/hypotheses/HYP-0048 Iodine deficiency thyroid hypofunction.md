# HYP-0048 Iodine deficiency thyroid hypofunction


Maternal iodine insufficiency during pregnancy disrupts fetal thyroid hormone, critical for neurodevelopment.


**Affected population:** Iodine-deficient regions


**Notes:** WHO classifies iodine deficiency as preventable cause of mental impairment.
