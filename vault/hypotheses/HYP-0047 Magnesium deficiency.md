# HYP-0047 Magnesium deficiency


Low RBC magnesium reported in autism; cofactor for NMDA regulation, ATP synthesis, GABA function. Common in Western diet patterns.


**Affected population:** Population-wide via diet


**Notes:** Often co-deficient with zinc.
