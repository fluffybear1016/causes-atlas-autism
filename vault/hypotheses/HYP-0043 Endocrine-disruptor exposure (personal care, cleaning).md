# HYP-0043 Endocrine-disruptor exposure (personal care, cleaning)


Phthalates, parabens, BPA in personal care products and household cleaners disrupt thyroid and sex hormone signaling during critical neurodevelopmental windows.


**Affected population:** Population-wide


**Notes:** Overlaps with HYP-0011 (phthalates) but broader exposure context.
