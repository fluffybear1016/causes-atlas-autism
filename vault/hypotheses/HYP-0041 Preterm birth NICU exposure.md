# HYP-0041 Preterm birth NICU exposure


Preterm infants (<32 weeks) have 2-4x autism risk; mechanisms include white matter injury, NICU environmental stress, impaired microbiome seeding.


**Affected population:** ~10% of autism cases attributable


**Notes:** Independent of other risk factors.
