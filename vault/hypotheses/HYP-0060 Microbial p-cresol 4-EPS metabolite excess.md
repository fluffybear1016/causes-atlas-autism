# HYP-0060 Microbial p-cresol 4-EPS metabolite excess


Elevated urinary and plasma p-cresol and 4-EPS in autism; mechanism via tyrosine metabolism by Clostridia. Inhibits catecholamine synthesis (Bermudez-Martin 2021); induces autism-like behavior in mice (Hsiao 2013).


**Affected population:** Subset with elevated metabolites


**Notes:** Strong mouse mechanistic; correlational human.
