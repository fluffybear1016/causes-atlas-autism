# HYP-0007 Gut microbiome dysbiosis dysbiotic fermentation


Distinct gut microbiome composition in autism; enriched Clostridia and depleted SCFA producers; microbial metabolites cross BBB and modulate behavior; FMT trials show durable improvement.


**Affected population:** Highly variable


**Notes:** Kang 2017+2019 FMT trials
