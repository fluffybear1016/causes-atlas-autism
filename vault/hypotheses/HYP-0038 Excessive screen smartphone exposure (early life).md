# HYP-0038 Excessive screen smartphone exposure (early life)


Heavy screen exposure in 0-3y associated with autism-like social-communication delays; mechanism debated (displacement of social input vs direct neurodevelopmental).


**Affected population:** Children with high (>2h/day) toddler screen time


**Notes:** Madigan 2020 JAMA Pediatrics.
