# HYP-0040 Birth complications (hypoxia, instrumental delivery)


Perinatal hypoxia, forceps/vacuum delivery, and prolonged labor associated with modestly increased autism risk via ischemic injury and inflammation.


**Affected population:** Complicated births subset


**Notes:** Effect size modest; confounded.
