# HYP-0058 Akkermansia Faecalibacterium depletion


Reduced Akkermansia muciniphila and Faecalibacterium prausnitzii (major butyrate producers) reported in autism cohorts. Both protect mucin layer and produce SCFAs.


**Affected population:** Subset; mucin barrier compromise


**Notes:** Pulikkan 2018; Liu 2022.
