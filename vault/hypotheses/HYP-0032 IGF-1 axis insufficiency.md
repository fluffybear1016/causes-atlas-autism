# HYP-0032 IGF-1 axis insufficiency


Low circulating IGF-1 reported in autism subsets, especially Rett (MECP2) and Phelan-McDermid (SHANK3). IGF-1 supplementation trials show signal in syndromic forms.


**Affected population:** Rett, Phelan-McDermid, idiopathic subset


**Notes:** IGF-1 trial ongoing in PMS.
