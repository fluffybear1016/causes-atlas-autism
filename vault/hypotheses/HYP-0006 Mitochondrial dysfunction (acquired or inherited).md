# HYP-0006 Mitochondrial dysfunction (acquired or inherited)


Subset of autism cases show elevated lactate/pyruvate, abnormal muscle biopsy, and respond to mitochondrial cocktails; may be primary or secondary to oxidative stress.


**Affected population:** ~30-50% of cases show mitochondrial markers


**Notes:** Frye + Rossignol consensus
