# HYP-0037 Childhood family emotional stress


Adverse childhood experiences and chronic family stress exacerbate autism phenotype severity (regression risk, behavioral dysregulation). Distinct from causation.


**Affected population:** Subset; modifies trajectory


**Notes:** Evidence largely correlational.
