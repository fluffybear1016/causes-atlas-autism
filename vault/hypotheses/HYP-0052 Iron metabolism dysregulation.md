# HYP-0052 Iron metabolism dysregulation


Iron deficiency anemia and ferritin abnormalities in autism subsets; iron critical for myelin, dopamine synthesis. Repletion shows behavioral benefit.


**Affected population:** Subset (Pica, restricted eating)


**Notes:** Reynolds 2020.
