# HYP-0057 Clostridia overgrowth dysbiotic fermentation


Elevated Clostridium species (esp. C. histolyticum group, C. perfringens) reported in autism cohorts. Clostridia produce p-cresol, 4-ethylphenyl sulfate, propionate at high levels — all linked to autism-like behavior in animal models.


**Affected population:** Subset, especially with GI symptoms


**Notes:** Finegold 2002, 2010; consume-GABA hypothesis.
