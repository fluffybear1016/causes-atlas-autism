# SS-31 (Elamipretide / MTP-131)

## Mechanism of action

4-amino-acid peptide (D-Arg-Dmt-Lys-Phe-NH2). Selectively binds cardiolipin on inner mitochondrial membrane; stabilizes electron-transport-chain super-complexes. Reduces mitochondrial ROS leak. In FDA review for Barth syndrome (mitochondrial). Stealth BioTherapeutics.
