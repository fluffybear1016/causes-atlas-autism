## Mechanism of action

6-amino-acid analog of angiotensin IV. Most potent known HGF (hepatocyte growth factor) mimetic. Promotes synaptogenesis + dendritic spine formation in preclinical models. Crosses BBB. Harding lab work; investigated for Alzheimer's.
