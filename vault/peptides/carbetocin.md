# Carbetocin

> Social Neuropeptide · evidence tier `mechanistic_only_autism`

**Atlas:** _not yet promoted — vault-only research scaffold; needs PMID-verified evidence for atlas promotion_

## Mechanism of action

Long-acting oxytocin analog (8-amino-acid synthetic). Selective OXTR agonist; greater receptor selectivity + biased agonism + longer half-life vs native oxytocin (40-60 min vs 3-10 min). FDA-approved for postpartum hemorrhage. Under investigation for Prader-Willi syndrome (intranasal LV-101 formulation).

## Safety profile

Long-acting profile risks more pronounced receptor desensitization than native oxytocin. Pediatric data limited to Prader-Willi studies. Cardiovascular caution (oxytocin-class hypotension).

## Formulations

- Intranasal (LV-101 investigational)
- IM/IV (obstetric)
