## Mechanism of action

28-amino-acid neuropeptide; sibling to PACAP. Acts on VPAC1 + VPAC2 receptors. Critical neurotrophic role in embryonic + early postnatal brain. Gozes lab work on VIP analogs (NAP, davunetide) for autism + Alzheimer's + schizophrenia.
