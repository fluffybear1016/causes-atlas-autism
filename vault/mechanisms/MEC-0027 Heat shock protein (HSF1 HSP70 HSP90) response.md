# MEC-0027 Heat shock protein (HSF1 HSP70 HSP90) response


**Notes:** Cellular stress-response chaperone system. Refolds misfolded proteins, prevents aggregation, modulates inflammation. Induced by heat (sauna), cold, exercise, sulforaphane. Hypothesized mechanism for the 'fever effect' in autism (Curran 2007).
