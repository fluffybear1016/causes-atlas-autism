# MEC-0031 DHA membrane phospholipid composition


**Notes:** DHA (22:6n-3) incorporated into neuronal phospholipids (phosphatidylserine, phosphatidylethanolamine). Determines membrane fluidity, receptor function, signaling. Phospholipid DHA accumulates ~10x more in brain than triglyceride DHA (Werner 2011). Distinct from neuroinflammation effects.
