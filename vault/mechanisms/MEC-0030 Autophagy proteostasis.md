# MEC-0030 Autophagy proteostasis


**Notes:** Cellular quality-control: degradation of damaged proteins and organelles. Activated by fasting, exercise, caloric restriction, rapamycin (mTOR inhibition), AMPK signaling. Disrupted in syndromic autism (TSC, PTEN — mTOR hyperactivation). Distinct from mTOR pathway itself (MEC-0009) — autophagy is downstream effector.
