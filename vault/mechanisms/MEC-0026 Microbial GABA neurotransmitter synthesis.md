# MEC-0026 Microbial GABA neurotransmitter synthesis


**Notes:** Specific Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium strains produce GABA, serotonin precursors, dopamine. Bifidobacterium-deficient autism cohorts may have reduced microbial GABA contribution.
