# MEC-0032 Aluminum adjuvant accumulation TLR-mediated neuroinflammation


**Notes:** Aluminum hydroxide / aluminum phosphate used as vaccine adjuvants activate NLRP3 inflammasome and TLR signaling. Hypothesized mechanism for any neurodevelopmental signal from cumulative aluminum exposure in early life. Distinct from existing heavy metal hypothesis (HYP-0015) — aluminum specifically and timing around vaccinations.
