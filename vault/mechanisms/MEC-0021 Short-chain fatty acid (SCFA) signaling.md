# MEC-0021 Short-chain fatty acid (SCFA) signaling


**Notes:** Butyrate, acetate, propionate produced by Faecalibacterium, Roseburia, Eubacterium. Modulate microglial maturation, BBB integrity, T-reg differentiation. Both protective (low dose) and toxic (high propionate animal models).
