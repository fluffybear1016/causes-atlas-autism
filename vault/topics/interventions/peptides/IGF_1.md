# IGF 1

Insulin-like growth factor 1 (mecasermin). FDA-approved for primary IGFD. Best evidence in **SHANK3 / Phelan-McDermid syndrome** — rescues synaptic protein synthesis defects. Per [[Hollander_Eric]] Phase 2 trials.
