# Exorphins Casomorphin Gluteomorphin

Opioid-like peptides from incomplete digestion of gluten/casein. Biological substrate for GFCF diet effect. Cross BBB in some children with intestinal hyperpermeability. Bind μ-opioid receptors; affect mood/behavior/sensory. Reichelt + Knivsberg foundational research.
