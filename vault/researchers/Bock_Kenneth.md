# Kenneth Bock, MD — The Bock Plan and the 4-A epidemic framework

Kenneth Bock is a board-certified family physician operating one of the longest-running functional/integrative pediatric practices in the United States (Bock Integrative Medicine, Red Hook NY and Manhattan). His autism contribution is twofold: a **clinical biomedical protocol** (often called the "Bock Plan") that has treated thousands of autistic children with measurable outcomes, and a **conceptual framework** — the "4-A epidemic" — that links autism, ADHD, asthma, and allergies as manifestations of shared environmental/immune/metabolic dysfunction in pediatric populations.

## Why he matters

Bock represents the clinical-volume side of functional medicine for autism. Where [[Frye_Richard]] runs RCTs and [[Hazan_Sabine]] runs precision microbiome cases, Bock has spent 30+ years actually treating thousands of autistic children with structured biomedical protocols (gut healing, methylation support, heavy metal detoxification, mitochondrial support, food sensitivity / inflammation reduction, neurotransmitter balance). The accumulated clinical experience, even though much of it isn't formally published, is significant.

His **4-A framework** ties autism to ADHD, asthma, and allergies as a unified phenomenon: all four show simultaneous, dramatic prevalence increases in the same generations, all four have established environmental contributors, and all four respond to overlapping biomedical interventions. This framing is consistent with [[Rogers_Toby]]'s political-economy framework and with the broader [[Hannah Poling framework]] that the atlas operates under.

## Key work

***Healing the New Childhood Epidemics: Autism, ADHD, Asthma, and Allergies* (2007, with later updates)** — Comprehensive synthesis of the 4-A framework and the clinical protocol. Densely referenced. The book that established Bock as a primary clinical-side voice in functional pediatrics.

***The Road to Immunity*** — Earlier work on immune system function and pediatric chronic disease.

**Clinical practice** — Currently active. Bock Integrative Medicine sees patients in person and via telehealth across multiple states. Workup includes comprehensive food sensitivity, metals, gut microbiome, methylation, mitochondrial markers, neurotransmitter analysis.

## Concept summary

**The 4-A framework.** Autism, ADHD, asthma, allergies — different presentations, shared roots in immune dysregulation, environmental load, gut barrier integrity, methylation defects, and mitochondrial vulnerability. A child who has one of the four is at substantially elevated risk of having or developing the others.

**Layered biomedical protocol.** Bock's clinical approach is structured rather than monotherapeutic. Foundational layer (food, sleep, environment), gut healing layer (microbiome, barrier integrity, food sensitivities), detoxification layer (heavy metals, glutathione, sulfation), methylation/mitochondrial layer (cofactors, reduced folate, methyl-B12, mito support), and immune/neurotransmitter layer as needed. Multi-domain rather than single-target.

**Individualization through testing.** Like [[Walsh_William]] and [[Frye_Richard]], Bock's framework is biomarker-driven, not algorithmic. Each child gets workup → stratification → targeted protocol → re-test → iterate.
