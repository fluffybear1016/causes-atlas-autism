## Evidence fragments


### EVD-001426 — result (positive)


> Wong CC et al. (2014, Mol Psychiatry, PMID 23608919): first systematic genome-wide DNA methylation analysis in 50 monozygotic twin pairs (100 individuals) discordant and concordant for ASD. 'Within-twin and between-group analyses identified numerous differentially methylated regions associated with ASD... significant correlations between DNA methylation and quantitatively measured autistic trait scores across our sample cohort.' First systematic epigenomic analysis of MZ twins discordant for ASD; implicates altered DNA methylation as non-genetic ASD driver.


**Supports:** hypothesis: [[HYP-0072 Epigenetic canalization failure]]; mechanism: [[MEC-0003 Impaired methylation]]
