### EVD-000678 — result (unclear)


> Epidemiological evidence indicates exposure to glyphosate-based herbicides (GBHs) increases the risk for autism spectrum disorder (ASD). The gut microbiota has been found to influence ASD behaviours through the microbiota-gut-brain axis. However, the underlying links between early life GBH exposure and ASD-like phenotypes through the microbiota-gut-brain axis remain unclear. Therefore, we exposed mice to low-dose GBH (0.10, 0.25, 0.50, and 1.00 %) and determined the effects on ASD-like behaviour
