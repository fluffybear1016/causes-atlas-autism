> Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) appears in early childhood and is characterized by persistent deficits in communication and social interaction, as well as restricted interests, repetitive behaviors, and unusual sensory issues. ASD can be idiopathic or syndromic, in the latter case representing one of the many manifestations of a genetic disorder, such as Rett syndrome. Psychopharmacology cannot directly ameliorate core autistic symptoms, but rather aims at treating comorbid disorders, such as ADH
