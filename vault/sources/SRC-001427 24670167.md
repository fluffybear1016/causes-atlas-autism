# SRC-001427 24670167


**Title:** Patches of disorganization in the neocortex of children with autism (Stoner 2014)


**URL:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/24670167/


**PMID:** 24670167


## Evidence fragments


### EVD-001427 — result (positive)


> Stoner R et al. (2014, NEJM, PMID 24670167): postmortem RNA in situ hybridization analysis with layer-/cell-type-specific markers in 11 children with autism vs 11 unaffected controls (ages 2-15). 'Focal patches of abnormal laminar cytoarchitecture and cortical disorganization of neurons, but not glia, in prefrontal and temporal cortical tissue from 10 of 11 children with autism and from 1 of 11 unaffected children.' Layers 4 and 5 most affected. Conclusion: 'probable dysregulation of layer formation and layer-specific neuronal differentiation at prenatal developmental stages.'


**Supports:** hypothesis: [[HYP-0073 Developmental timing state-transition disorder]]; mechanism: [[MEC-0006 Synaptic pruning abnormalities]]
