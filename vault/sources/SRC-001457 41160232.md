# SRC-001457 41160232


**Title:** Multi-omics reveals cross-tissue regulatory mechanisms of autism risk loci via gut microbiota-immunity-brain axis.


**URL:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41160232/


**PMID:** 41160232


**Notes / methodological caveats:** Liao 2025 AMB Express — cross-tissue multi-omics integrating ASD GWAS (12.6M SNPs, 47 novel risk loci) + multi-region eQTL + epigenetic + microbiome data. Identifies cross-scale axis: genetic variation → epigenetic regulation → neuronal cell function. Strongest eQTL enrichment in cerebral cortex. Forward MR on 473 gut taxa identifies 24 with causal ASD associations. Tight junction pathway emerges as cross-tissue mechanism connecting genetic risk and microbiota.
