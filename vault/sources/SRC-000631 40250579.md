### EVD-000631 — result (unclear)


> Previous epidemiologic studies on gestational chemical exposures and autism spectrum disorder (ASD) often lack analysis of chemical mixtures or are limited to investigating certain chemical classes. We examined the impact of multi-class chemical mixtures on ASD risk, using data from the MARBLES (Markers of Autism Risks in Babies-Learning Early Signs) cohort. Children were clinically assessed at age 3 and classified as ASD, typical development (TD), or non-TD with other neurodevelopmental concern
