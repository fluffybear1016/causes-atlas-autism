### EVD-001423 — result (positive)


> Ben-Ari Y (2014, Neuroscience, PMID 25168736): foundational personal review of the GABA excitatory/inhibitory developmental sequence. 'We observed elevated intracellular chloride [Cl-]i levels and excitatory GABA early during development and a perinatal excitatory/inhibitory shift. This sequence is observed in a wide range of brain structures and animal species suggesting that it has been conserved throughout evolution. It is mediated primarily by a developmentally regulated expression of the NKCC1 and KCC2 chloride importer and exporter respectively.' Sequences can be deviated in utero by genetic or environmental insults leading to persistence of immature features in adult brain. Therapeutic perspective: drugs blocking immature but not adult currents.
