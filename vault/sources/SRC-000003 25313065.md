# SRC-000003 25313065


**Title:** Sulforaphane treatment of autism spectrum disorder (ASD)


**URL:** https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.1416940111

## Evidence fragments


### EVD-000003 — result (positive)


> Forty-four young men with moderate-to-severe ASD received sulforaphane (50-150 µmol) or placebo for 18 weeks. Substantial improvements in irritability, lethargy, repetitive behavior, and social responsiveness in treatment group.
