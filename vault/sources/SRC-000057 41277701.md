### EVD-000057 — result (unclear)


> Folate compounds are crucial for DNA and RNA synthesis, homocysteine regulation, and epigenetic methylation. However, significant differences exist between 5-methyltetrahydrofolate (5-MTHF) and folinic acid (CHO-THF) -and synthetic folic acid (sFA). Understanding their absorption, bioavailability, and clinical effects is essential, especially for women planning pregnancy, pregnant women, and patients with MTHFR or DHFR polymorphisms, autism spectrum disorders, or other folate-related conditions.
