### EVD-000376 — result (unclear)


> Antidiabetic medications have shown efficacy in alleviating autism symptoms. However, there is a lack of clinical research on the impact of metformin on irritability associated with autism. This study aimed to assess the efficacy and safety of metformin as an adjuvant therapy with risperidone for managing irritability in children diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). This is a randomized, 10-week, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial conducted at the children's autism clinic of Roozbe
