# SRC-001456 40400531


**Title:** Causal relationship between gut microbiota, blood metabolites and autism spectrum disorder: a Mendelian randomization study.


**URL:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40400531/


**PMID:** 40400531


**Notes / methodological caveats:** Wang 2025 R Soc Open Sci — two-sample MR establishing genetically-instrumented causal chain: gut microbiota → blood metabolites → ASD. 15 taxa causally associated with ASD; 52 metabolites causally associated; mediation analysis identifies 9 metabolites mediating microbiota→ASD effects (e.g., 1-methyl-5-imidazoleacetate mediates ~12% of A. fermentans protective effect; pyridoxate mediates ~12% of Lachnospirales effect). Methodologically strong: explicit causal inference framework with sensitivity tests excluding pleiotropy. Limited to European cohorts.
