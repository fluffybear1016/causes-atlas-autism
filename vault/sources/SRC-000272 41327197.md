### EVD-000272 — result (unclear)


> Antiemetics are commonly prescribed for nausea and vomiting during pregnancy (NVP), affecting up to 80% of pregnant women. However, data on their long-term neurodevelopmental effects are limited. We therefore aimed to assess the risk of neurodevelopmental disorders and delays in children exposed to antiemetics commonly prescribed during pregnancy. This nationwide cohort study, designed to emulate a pragmatic trial, used South Korea's National Health Insurance Service mother-child linked database
