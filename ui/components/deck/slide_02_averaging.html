Slide 2 · The averaging problem

Population-average research systematically dilutes biologically distinct responder subgroups.

A treatment showing “no significant effect” in aggregate may conceal a strong effect in a biologically coherent subgroup.

This failure mode is not occasional. It is structural to the meta-analytic methods that dominate published evidence synthesis.

Mainstream meta-analytic methods cannot resolve mechanistic heterogeneity.
